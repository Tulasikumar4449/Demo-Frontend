## How to Join Saheya: Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Create Your Saheya Account

**Online Registration:**
1. Visit the Saheya website or download the Saheya Worker app
2. Click on "Register as Worker" or "Join as Worker"
3. Provide basic information:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - Password
   - City/Location

**Select Your Services:**
Choose the services you offer from our 14 service categories. You can select multiple services based on your expertise.

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile

After initial registration, you'll need to complete your detailed profile:

**Personal Information:**
- Full legal name (as per government ID)
- Date of birth
- Gender
- Complete address with PIN code
- Years of experience in your field

**Professional Details:**
- Service categories you specialize in
- Hourly rate or fixed pricing for services
- Working hours and availability
- Service areas (localities you can serve)

**Profile Photo:**
Upload a clear, recent photograph for your profile (this will be visible to customers)

### Step 3: Document Submission & KYC Verification

**Required Documents:**

1. **Identity Proof** (Any one):
   - Aadhaar Card
   - PAN Card
   - Voter ID
   - Passport
   - Driver's License

2. **Address Proof** (Any one):
   - Aadhaar Card
   - Utility Bill (Electricity/Water/Gas) - not older than 3 months
   - Rental Agreement
   - Property Tax Receipt

3. **Professional Certificates** (If applicable):
   - Trade certificates
   - Training completion certificates
   - Professional licenses
   - FSSAI certificate (for Chef & Cooking Services)
   - Medical license/certification (for Medical & Well Being Services)
   - Driving license (for Drivers Services)

4. **Bank Account Details:**
   - Bank account number
   - IFSC code
   - Branch name
   - Cancelled cheque or bank statement

5. **Police Verification Certificate:**
   - Submit police verification clearance from your local police station
   - Saheya can facilitate this process through partner police stations

6. **References:**
   - Provide 2-3 professional or personal references
   - Include their contact information for verification

### Step 4: Background Verification

Saheya conducts a comprehensive background check:

**Verification Process:**
1. **Document Verification:** Our team verifies all submitted documents for authenticity
2. **Police Verification:** Cross-check with police records for any criminal history
3. **Reference Check:** Contact your provided references to verify character and work ethic
4. **Skill Assessment:** For certain services, you may need to complete a practical skill test
5. **In-Person Interview:** A Saheya representative may visit your location for face-to-face verification

**Timeline:** The verification process typically takes 5-7 working days.

### Step 5: Training & Onboarding

Once verified, you'll go through our onboarding program:

**Orientation Session:**
- Understanding the Saheya platform
- Customer service best practices
- Safety protocols and guidelines
- Using the Saheya Worker app
- Pricing and payment processes

**Service-Specific Training:**
- Quality standards for your service category
- Latest tools and techniques
- Customer interaction guidelines

### Step 6: Sign the Partnership Agreement

A digital partnership agreement will be sent to you covering:
- Terms and conditions
- Commission structure
- Code of conduct
- Service quality standards
- Cancellation policies
- Dispute resolution mechanism

Review and sign the agreement electronically through the app.

### Step 7: Receive Your Saheya Verification Kit

After completing all steps, you'll receive:
- **Saheya Worker ID Card** with your photo and unique ID
- **Verification Badge** for your profile
- **Welcome Kit** with branded materials (optional)
- **App Access** to start receiving job requests

### Step 8: Start Accepting Jobs

You're now ready to:
- Set your availability
- Browse available jobs in your area
- Accept or decline job requests
- Start earning through Saheya

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does the verification process take?**
A: Typically 5-7 working days, depending on document verification and police clearance.

**Q: Can I work in multiple cities?**
A: Yes, but you need to complete verification for each city and maintain service quality standards.

**Q: What if a customer doesn't pay?**
A: Saheya handles all payments. Customers pay through the platform, and you receive your share regardless.

**Q: Can I reject jobs?**
A: Yes, you have full control over which jobs to accept. However, maintaining a good acceptance rate improves your profile.

**Q: Is there a joining fee?**
A: No, joining Saheya is completely free. We only charge commission on completed jobs.

**Q: What happens if a customer complains?**
A: Our support team investigates all complaints. You'll have a chance to explain your side before any action is taken.

**Q: Can I increase my prices?**
A: Yes, you can update your pricing anytime. However, competitive pricing attracts more customers.

**Q: How do I track my earnings?**
A: The Worker app provides real-time earnings dashboard with detailed transaction history.
